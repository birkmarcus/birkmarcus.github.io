ABOUT

Older is a French/Danish fashion house based in Paris with manufacturing and production in Italy. Older was founded by Morten Thuesen in 2013. Born in the countryside of Denmark in 1988, Morten Thuesen studied at the London College Of Fashion before moving to Paris. The Danish born Parisian based designer has been working in the studios of French Couturist Josephus Thimister, German-Croatian new thinker Damir Doma and British fashion's enfant terrible, Alexander McQueen.
